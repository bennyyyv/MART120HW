Hey! My name's Ben. I'm from White Bear Lake Minnesota. I first became interested in photography when my dad let me use his camera on a family vacation to Glacier National Park. After that first encounter with a camera I've been hooked on capturing all the adventures I go on in the outdoors. Currently, I'm attending the University of Montana in Missoula and majoring in Media Arts with a focus in Digital Art. I am also a member of the University of Montana cross country and track and field teams.
